# Contract Review Quick Scan

Fast contract assessment for agreements governed by Chinese law.

---

## Quick Red Flags Check

| Red Flag | Status | Issue |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Unusual terms never seen before | ⬜ | May require legal counsel |
| Heavily one-sided terms | ⬜ | Unfair to one party |
| Ambiguous language creating uncertainty | ⬜ | Interpretation risk |
| Inconsistent clauses (self-conflicting) | ⬜ | Contract conflicts with itself |
| Automatic renewal without clear notice | ⬜ | Consumer protection issue |
| Unclear IP ownership | ⬜ | Future dispute risk |
| Foreign law governing clause | ⬜ | Should specify PRC law |
| Not in Chinese language | ⬜ | May be deemed invalid |

**If 3+ red flags → Recommend legal counsel**

## Essential Clauses by Contract Type

### Franchise Agreement (特许经营)

| Clause | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Territorial exclusivity | Is exclusivity reasonable? |
| Royalty/fees | Are fees transparent and reasonable? |
| Trademark use | Quality control requirements? |
| Training obligations | Are obligations realistic? |
| Minimum performance | Are targets achievable? |
| Renewal terms | Fair to both parties? |

### Employment Contract (劳动合同)

| Clause | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Salary & payment | Meets minimum wage, paid on time? |
| Working hours | Complies with 40h standard? |
| Non-compete | Reasonable scope and duration? |
| Social insurance | Employer contributes (社保)? |
| Probation | ≤6 months (2 for some roles)? |
| IP assignment | Covers work-created IP? |

### Partnership Agreement (合伙协议)

| Clause | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Profit distribution | Clear formula? |
| Decision-making | How are decisions made? |
| Withdrawal | Can partners withdraw with notice? |
| Death/incapacity | Handling of partner death/disability? |
| Dispute resolution | Mediation before litigation? |

### User Subscription Terms (用户订阅)

| Clause | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Price transparency | Terms visible before purchase? |
| Cancellation policy | Clear cancellation process? |
| Refund terms | 7-day minimum refund? |
| No hidden fees | Transparent pricing? |
| Payment data | NOT stored (WeChat Pay compliance)? |

## China-Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Law/Regulation |
|-------------|----------------|
| Written employment contract | Labor Contract Law (劳动合同法) |
| Social insurance contributions | Labor Contract Law (劳动合同法) |
| Consumer protection | Consumer Rights Law (消费者权益保护法) |
| 7-day cooling-off (franchise) | Franchise Regulations (商业特许经营管理条例) |
| Chinese language for contracts | Consumer Rights, E-commerce Law |
| PRC governing law | Civil Code (民法典) |

## Common Contract Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Consequence | Fix |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Not in Chinese | May be invalid | Provide bilingual version |
| No governing law | Unclear which law applies | Specify "受中华人民共和国法律管辖" |
| Unequal termination rights | Unenforceable | Allow termination for material breach |
| Waiving consumer protections | Void provisions | Remove prohibited waivers |
| Missing required clauses | Incomplete agreement | Add based on contract type |

## When to Escalate to Legal Counsel

**Immediately escalate for:**
1. Government inquiry or official contact
2. Lawsuit or legal threat
3. Platform suspension or account ban
4. Complex cross-border issues
5. Criminal matters
6. Unclear or conflicting regulations
7. High-stakes transactions (large contracts, acquisitions)
8. Criminal liability concerns

## Bilingual Considerations

| Language | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Chinese (中文)** | Official legal text for Chinese courts |
| **English** | Reference for foreign parties |

**Important:** In case of conflict, Chinese version typically prevails in Chinese courts. Make this explicit in the contract.
