# Consumer Rights Protection Guide

**Source:** Law of the PRC on Protection of Consumer Rights and Interests (2013 Amendment)
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## Consumer Rights Protection Law - Contract Clauses Guide

---

## Essential Consumer Protection Clauses

### 1. 7-Day Cooling-Off Period (Article 25)

**Required for:** Online purchases, digital services

**Contract language:**
```
消费者自收到商品之日起七日内可以无理由退货。
Consumers may return goods within seven days of receipt without reason.
```

**Exceptions to disclose:**
- Customized products
- Perishable goods
- Digital downloads (after consumption)
- Newspapers/periodicals

### 2. Refund Policy (Article 24)

**Required elements:**
- Clear refund timeline
- Refund method
- Any restocking fees
- Process for requesting refund

**Sample language:**
```
退款将在收到退货后[X]个工作日内处理。
Refunds will be processed within [X] working days after receipt of return.
```

### 3. Cancellation Rights (Article 26)

**Auto-renewal contracts must:**
- Disclose auto-renewal before subscription
- Provide clear notice before each renewal
- Allow easy cancellation
- Not require more than reasonable notice

**Required disclosure:**
```
本服务将自动续费，您可以在账户设置中随时取消。
This service will automatically renew; you may cancel at any time in account settings.
```

### 4. Price Transparency (Article 8)

**Must display before purchase:**
- Total price including all fees
- Subscription renewal amount
- Any additional charges
- Currency and payment frequency

### 5. Data Protection (Article 14, 29)

**Contract must state:**
- What data is collected
- Purpose of collection
- How data is protected
- User data rights
- Contact for privacy concerns

## Unfair Contract Terms (Article 26)

The following clauses are **NULL AND VOID:**

### ❌ Invalid Clauses
1. Excluding or limiting consumer liability
2. Excluding or limiting merchant liability for personal injury
3. Excluding or limiting merchant liability for property damage
4. Exempting merchant from statutory obligations
5. Increasing consumer obligations beyond law
6. Excluding or limiting consumer's right to seek remedies
7. Forcing consumers to accept unfavorable terms
8. Changing terms unilaterally without consent
9. Single-party termination rights favoring merchant
10. Waiving consumer protection rights

### ✅ Valid Alternatives
- Clear description of services
- Fair limitation of liability (mutual)
- Reasonable cancellation notice periods
- Clear pricing and fees
- Dispute resolution mechanism

## Punitive Damages (Article 55)

For **fraudulent** conduct against consumers:
- **3x** the purchase price as punitive damages
- Plus actual damages
- Consumer bears burden of proving fraud

**What constitutes fraud:**
- Intentional misrepresentation
- Concealing material facts
- False advertising
- Selling counterfeit goods

## Digital Platform Specific Requirements

### Mini Programs / Apps
| Requirement | Source |
|-------------|--------|
| Accessible privacy policy | PIPL + WeChat rules |
| 7-day refund for new users | Consumer Rights Law |
| Clear cancellation | Auto-renewal rules |
| No hidden fees | Price transparency |
| Contact information | Service obligations |

### Subscription Services
| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Auto-renewal disclosure | Before subscription |
| Pre-renewal notice | 3-7 days before |
| Easy cancellation | In-app, not phone/email only |
| Refund policy | 7-day minimum |

## Common Contract Issues

| Issue | Legal Position | Fix |
|-------|---------------|-----|
| "All sales final" | **Void** for online purchases | Add 7-day return policy |
| "No refunds" | **Void** | Allow 7-day refund |
| "We may change terms anytime" | **Void** | Require notice, allow opt-out |
| "You waive all rights" | **Void** | Remove, use fair language |
| Hidden fees | **Illegal** | Disclose all fees upfront |
| Complex cancellation | **Questionable** | Simplify to 1-2 clicks |

## Template Clauses

### Right to Cancel
```
You may cancel your subscription at any time through the app settings.
Cancellation will take effect immediately, and you will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
您可以随时通过应用设置取消订阅。取消将立即生效，您将不会被收取下一个计费周期的费用。
```

### Refund Policy
```
New subscribers may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
After 7 days, refunds are prorated based on remaining subscription period.
新订阅者可以在购买后7天内申请全额退款。
7天后，退款将根据剩余订阅期按比例计算。
```

### Liability Limitation (Fair)
```
We are liable for damages caused by our intentional misconduct or gross negligence.
We are not liable for indirect damages, lost profits, or data loss beyond our control.
我们对故意不当行为或重大过失造成的损害承担责任。
我们不对间接损害、利润损失或超出我们控制范围的数据丢失承担责任。
```
