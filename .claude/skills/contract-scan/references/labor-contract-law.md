# Labor Contract Law Key Provisions

**Source:** Labor Contract Law of the PRC (2008, 2012 amendment)
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## Labor Contract Law of the PRC - Essential Articles

**Effective:** January 1, 2008
**Amended:** December 28, 2012

---

## Essential Contract Clauses (Article 17)

A labor contract **shall contain** the following essential clauses:

1. **Employer Information**
   - Name, domicile
   - Legal representative or principal person

2. **Employee Information**
   - Name, address
   - ID number (or other valid ID)

3. **Contract Term** (合同期限)
   - Fixed-term
   - Open-ended
   - Task-based

4. **Job Description** (工作内容)
   - Position/title
   - Job duties

5. **Work Location** (工作地点)
   - Where work is performed

6. **Working Hours, Rest & Vacation** (工作时间和休息休假)
   - Standard: 40 hours/week
   - Overtime rules
   - Paid annual leave

7. **Labor Remuneration** (劳动报酬)
   - Salary amount
   - Payment schedule (monthly)
   - Overtime pay

8. **Social Insurance** (社会保险) ⭐ MANDATORY
   - Pension
   - Medical
   - Unemployment
   - Work injury
   - Maternity

9. **Occupational Protection** (劳动保护)
   - Working conditions
   - Safety measures
   - Hazard protection

10. **Other Legal Requirements**

## Probation Period Rules (Article 19)

| Contract Term | Maximum Probation |
|---------------|-------------------|
| < 3 months | **Not allowed** |
| 3 months to < 1 year | **1 month** |
| 1 year to < 3 years | **2 months** |
| ≥ 3 years / Open-ended | **6 months** |

### Key Probation Rules (Article 19-21)
- Only **one** probation period with the same employee
- Probation must be **included within** the contract term
- **Probation wages:** At least 80% of agreed wage, not below minimum wage
- Separate probation contract → probation becomes contract term

---

## Social Insurance Requirements (Article 17, 50)

### Mandatory Contributions
Both employer AND employee must contribute:

| Type | Employer | Employee |
|------|----------|----------|
| Pension | ~16-20% | ~8% |
| Medical | ~6-10% | ~2% |
| Unemployment | ~1-2% | ~0.5-1% |
| Work Injury | ~0.5-2% | 0% |
| Maternity | ~0.5-1% | 0% |

**Rates vary by city.** Local regulations apply.

### Employer Violations (Article 74-75)
- Failure to pay social insurance: Administrative fines
- Failure to provide social insurance statement: Employee may terminate

## Non-Compete Clauses (Article 23-24)

### Requirements for Valid Non-Compete:
1. **Written agreement** required
2. **Compensation** required during non-compete period
3. **Limited scope:** Same or similar business
4. **Limited duration:** Maximum **2 years**
5. **Limited geography:** Region where employer does business

### Compensation Standards
- Monthly economic compensation required
- Amount varies by region (typically 30%+ of average monthly salary)
- Failure to pay → employee may terminate non-compete

## Termination (Articles 36-44)

### Employee May Terminate (Article 37):
- 30 days written notice (fixed-term)
- 3 days notice (probation)

### Employer May Terminate (Article 39):
- Serious violation of rules
- Gross negligence, causing damage
- Establishing employment relationship elsewhere
- Criminal prosecution

### Employer May Terminate with Notice (Article 40):
- Incompetence after training (30 days notice + 1 month pay)
- Major change in circumstances, contract cannot be performed
- Illness/non-work injury, unable to work after medical leave

### Severance Pay (Article 46-47)
- Required for certain employer-initiated terminations
- Amount: 1 month salary per year of service
- Caps: 12 years (local variations)
- 6 months to <1 year = 1 month
- <6 months = 0.5 month

## Contract Types

### Fixed-Term Contract (固定期限)
- Specified end date
- Can be renewed
- After 2 consecutive fixed-term contracts → must offer open-ended

### Open-Ended Contract (无固定期限)
- No specified end date
- More job security
- Mandatory after:
  - 10 years of service
  - 2 consecutive fixed-term contracts
  - Employee requests (with 10+ years or other conditions)

### Task-Based Contract (以完成一定工作任务为期限)
- Ends when task completed
- Less common

## Red Flags in Employment Contracts

| Clause | Legal Position |
|--------|---------------|
| No social insurance contribution | **Illegal** (Article 17) |
| Probation > 6 months | **Illegal** (Article 19) |
| Probation outside contract | **Illegal** (Article 19) |
| Waiving labor rights | **Void** (public policy) |
| No overtime pay | **Illegal** (unless specific exemptions) |
| Excessive non-compete (2+ years) | **Unenforceable** |
| Non-compete without compensation | **Unenforceable** |
