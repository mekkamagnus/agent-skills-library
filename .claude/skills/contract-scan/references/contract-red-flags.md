## Common Contract Red Flags Under Chinese Law

---

## Critical Red Flags (Immediate Action Required)

### 1. Language Issues
| Issue | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| Not in Chinese | May be deemed invalid in Chinese courts |
| No governing law clause | Unclear which law applies |
| Inconsistent translations | Chinese version typically prevails |

**Fix:** Add bilingual clause: "本合同以中文为准。中文版本与英文版本不一致时，以中文版本为准。"

### 2. Governing Law
| Issue | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| Foreign law specified | May not be enforceable in China |
| No governing law clause | Unclear jurisdiction |
| Foreign arbitration | May not be recognized |

**Standard:** "受中华人民共和国法律管辖"

### 3. Unfair Terms
| Issue | Why It Matters |
|-------|----------------|
| One-sided termination | Unequal termination rights |
| Excessive penalties | May be unenforceable |
| No liability for merchant | Violates consumer protection |
| Waiving statutory rights | Void under Chinese law |

## Employment Contract Red Flags

| Red Flag | Legal Position | Risk Level |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| No social insurance contribution | Illegal (Article 17) | HIGH |
| Probation > 6 months | Illegal (Article 19) | HIGH |
| Probation outside contract term | Illegal | HIGH |
| Probation wages < 80% | Illegal | HIGH |
| No written contract | Employer fined (2x salary) | HIGH |
| Non-compete > 2 years | Unenforceable | MEDIUM |
| Non-compete without compensation | Unenforceable | MEDIUM |
| "No overtime" clause | Questionable (unless specific exemptions) | MEDIUM |

## Franchise Agreement Red Flags

| Red Flag | Legal Position | Risk Level |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| Franchisor not registered LLC | Violates Article 7 | CRITICAL |
| <2 directly operated stores | Violates Article 7 | HIGH |
| <1 year of operation | Violates Article 7 | HIGH |
| No 30-day disclosure | Violates Article 21 | HIGH |
| No SAMR filing | Administrative violation | MEDIUM |
| No cooling-off period | May violate local law | MEDIUM |
| Automatic renewal without clear notice | Consumer protection issue | MEDIUM |
| Unclear IP ownership | Future dispute risk | HIGH |

## Standard Terms Red Flags

### General Commercial Contracts
| Issue | Risk Level |
|-------|------------|
| Automatic renewal without clear notice | MEDIUM |
| Unclear IP ownership | HIGH |
| No limitation of liability (unbalanced) | MEDIUM |
| No force majeure clause | MEDIUM |
| No dispute resolution mechanism | LOW |
| Confidentiality clause missing | MEDIUM |
| No termination clause | MEDIUM |

### Always-Prohibited Clauses (Void)
- Excluding liability for personal injury
- Excluding liability for intentional misconduct
- Excluding statutory consumer rights
- Unilateral termination without cause
- Waiving right to seek legal remedies

## Escalation Triggers

**Recommend legal counsel immediately for:**
1. Government inquiry or official contact
2. Lawsuit or legal threat
3. Platform suspension or account ban
4. Complex cross-border issues
5. High-stakes transactions (>¥500,000)
6. Criminal liability concerns
7. Unclear or conflicting regulations
8. Multiple red flags (3+)

---

## Quick Assessment Checklist

Before signing, check:

- [ ] Contract in Chinese (or bilingual)
- [ ] Governing law: PRC law
- [ ] Fair and balanced terms
- [ ] No illegal clauses (social insurance, probation limits)
- [ ] Clear rights and obligations
- [ ] Reasonable termination rights
- [ ] Dispute resolution mechanism
- [ ] IP ownership clear

**If 3+ red flags → Legal counsel recommended**
