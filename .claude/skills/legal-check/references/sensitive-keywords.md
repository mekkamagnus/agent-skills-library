# Sensitive Keywords Reference

**Source:** Compiled from CAC content moderation guidelines and WeChat platform policies
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## High-Risk Keyword Categories

### Political (Always Avoid)
- Government criticism terms
- Leadership names in negative context
- Political movement references
- Democracy advocacy
- Human rights campaigning
- Taiwan/Xinjiang/Tibet independence language

### Religious (Prohibited in Commercial Context)
- Proselytizing language
- Religious recruitment
- Sectarian terminology
- Falun Gong references
- Underground church terms

### Social Issues (Use Carefully)
- Protest-related terms
- Labor organization language
- Petition/campaign terminology
- Social injustice framing

### Always-Prohibited Terms
- Any term related to overthrowing state power
- Terrorist organization language
- Separatist movement terminology
- Rumor-mongering phrases

## Safe Language Alternatives

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| "民主运动" (democracy movement) | "community participation" |
| "维权" (rights defense) | "customer service" |
| "宗教" (religious) | "cultural" / "traditional" |
| "教会" (church) | "community" / "gathering" |
| "教育" (education) | "training" / "practice" (for fitness) |

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

**WeChat:** Stricter on political/religious content
**Douyin:** Focus on content quality over keywords
**Bilibili:** Educational context more tolerated

**Note:** This is general guidance. Always refer to official platform policies for current standards.
