# Prohibited Content Guidelines

**Source:** https://developers.weixin.qq.com/miniprogram/product/reject
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## WeChat Mini Program Prohibited Content Categories

### 1. Inducement & Manipulative Behavior (3.2.1)
- Inducing users to share, add, follow accounts, or download apps
- Content that requires sharing/adding/following to operate
- Explicit or implicit prompts to share (copy, images, buttons, floating layers, pop-ups)
- Using incentives or exaggerated language to coerce users to share

### 2. Excessive Marketing/Advertising (3.2.2)
- Pages primarily for marketing or advertising purposes
- Blank advertising slots, investment ads
- Floating/suspended ads
- Ad display ratio exceeding 50% in functional pages
- Ads that obstruct functionality

### 3. Misleading Content & Fear Tactics (3.2.3)
- Content that misleads users
- Content causing fear or psychological distress
- Rumors that seriously damage user experience or harm user interests

### 4. Test/Divination Content (3.2.4)
- Fortune-telling, lot-drawing, horoscope predictions
- Testing/quiz-type content

### 5. Fraudulent Content (3.2.5)
- Fake red envelopes (Hongbao)
- Fake activities/events
- Selling products that infringe on others' rights
- Impersonating Tencent official or other businesses
- Content that may confuse WeChat users

### 6. Spam & Harassment (3.2.6)
- Spreading harassment information
- Advertisements and spam messages

### 7. Legal & Political Violations (3.2.8)
- Content opposing constitutional principles
- Endangering national security, leaking state secrets
- Subverting state power, destroying national unity
- Damaging national honor and interests
- Fake or fraudulent content
- Content that advocates/encourages crime
- Anti-government or anti-social content
- Seditious political speech
- Rumors that disrupt social order

### 8. Pornographic Content (3.2.8.6)
- Pornographic material designed to arouse sexual desire
- Explicit descriptions or displays of sexual organs or behaviors
- Content that promotes/propagates obscenity
- Exposed images, provocative content
- Illegal sexual transaction information

### 9. Service Scope Violations (3.1.3-3.1.8)
- Services outside the approved category scope
- Hidden or paid features containing prohibited content
- Excessively simple functions (single page/button)
- Unauthorized replication of WeChat client features (Moments, Drift Bottle, etc.)
- Unauthorized third-party mini program promotion/navigation

### 10. Special Protections (3.11-3.12)
- **Minor Protection**: Inappropriate content for minors must include warnings
- **Objectification**: Pure commercialization of appearance/identity (beauty galleries, video collections, etc.)

## CAC Prohibited Content Standards

The Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC) prohibits:
- Political content opposing state authority
- Religious proselytizing
- Content that undermines ethnic unity
- Rumors and false information
- Pornography and obscenity
- Gambling and violence
- Content that endangers national security
