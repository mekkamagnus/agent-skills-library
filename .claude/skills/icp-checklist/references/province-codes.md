## ICP Number Format

```
[Province Code]ICP备[Year][Serial]号
```

**Examples:**
- `京ICP备20251234号` - Beijing, filed in 2025
- `沪ICP备202512345号` - Shanghai, filed in 2025
- `粤ICP备2025123456号` - Guangdong, filed in 2025

**Note:** The number of digits varies. Newer filings may have longer serial numbers.

## Filing Location

ICP filings are processed by the **local communications bureau** (通信管理局) in the province where:
- The business is registered, OR
- The server is located, OR
- The main operations are based

**Choose:** Where the business license is registered.

---

## Province Selection for LLCs

For companies with a business license, use the province of registration:

1. Check business license (营业执照)
2. Find "住所" (address) field
3. Extract the province/municipality
4. Match to the code table above

**Example:**
- Business license: "北京市朝阳区..."
- Province code: 京
- ICP number prefix: 京ICP备
