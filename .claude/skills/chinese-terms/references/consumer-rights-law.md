## Law of the PRC on Protection of Consumer Rights and Interests (2013 Amendment)

---

## Key Articles for Digital Platforms

### Article 8 - Consumer Right to Know
Consumers have the right to know the true circumstances of the commodities they purchase and use.

**Application:** Must provide clear product/service descriptions, pricing, and terms.

### Article 9 - Consumer Right to Choose
Consumers have the right to choose commodities freely and make purchase decisions independently.

**Application:** No forced bundling, auto-renewal without clear disclosure.

### Article 25 - Online Shopping Returns
For goods purchased online, consumers have the right to return goods within **seven days** without reason.

**Application:** 7-day cooling-off period for digital products/services.

### Article 26 - Unfair Contract Terms Null
Standard contract terms that exclude or limit consumer rights are **null and void**.

**Application:** Cannot waive consumer protections in user agreements.

### Article 28 - Online Platform Liability
If a consumer's rights are infringed through an online trading platform and the platform fails to provide the operator's true identity, the platform is **liable**.

### Article 44 - Consumer Personal Information Protection
Business operators and their staff must keep consumer personal information **confidential**.

**Application:** Must implement data security measures, not share/sell user data.

### Article 55 - Fraud Penalties
Fraud against consumers requires **punitive damages** of 3x the purchase price.

## Digital Service Specific Requirements

### 7-Day Cooling-Off (Article 25)
- Applies to: online purchases, digital services
- Must honor: unconditional returns within 7 days
- Exceptions: customized products, digital downloads (consumed)

### Refund Policy Requirements
1. Clear disclosure before purchase
2. Easy cancellation process
3. Refund within 7 days for new subscribers
4. Pro-rated refunds after use

### Auto-Renewal Disclosures
1. Must be disclosed before subscription
2. Clear notice before each renewal
3. Easy cancellation method
4. No hidden fees

## Penalty Framework

| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Consumer fraud | 3x punitive damages + actual damages |
| Personal data breach | Administrative fines up to ¥500,000 |
| Unfair contract terms | Terms declared null & void |
| Refusal to honor returns | Refund + compensation |
