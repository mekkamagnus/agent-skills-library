## Privacy Policy Template (PIPL-Compliant)

```markdown
# [App Name] 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]
**Effective Date:** [Date]

## 1. Information We Collect (我们收集的信息)

### 1.1 Personal Data (个人信息)
- User ID (用户唯一标识)
- Profile information (昵称、头像)
- Usage data (使用数据)
- User-generated content (用户上传内容)

### 1.2 Data Collection Purpose (收集目的)
- Provide personalized content (提供个性化内容)
- Track progress/activity (跟踪进度/活动)
- Send relevant notifications (发送相关通知)
- Process payments (处理支付)
- Improve services (改进服务)

### 1.3 Legal Basis (法律依据)
- Consent (用户同意)
- Contract performance (合同履行)
- Legal obligation (法律义务)

## 2. How We Use Data (数据使用)
- Service delivery (服务交付)
- Progress tracking (进度跟踪)
- Communication (沟通通知)
- Analytics (分析改进)

## 3. Data Storage (数据存储)
- **China-based servers** (中国境内服务器)
- Active users: While account active
- Inactive users: 3 years after last activity
- Deleted users: Immediately upon request

## 4. Your Rights (您的权利)
- Access (访问权)
- Correction (更正权)
- Deletion (删除权)
- Withdraw Consent (撤回同意)

## 5. Contact (联系我们)
- Email: [contact@email.com]
- In-app: [Contact method]
```

## Terms of Service Template

```markdown
# [App Name] 服务条款 / Terms of Service

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Service Nature (服务性质)
[App Name] is a [fitness/cultural/social] platform.

## 2. User Responsibilities (用户责任)
- Keep credentials secure (保管好凭证)
- Report unauthorized access (报告未授权访问)

### 2.1 Acceptable Use (可接受使用)
- ✅ [Allowed activities]
- ❌ Political content (政治内容)
- ❌ Religious proselytizing (宗教传教)
- ❌ Harassment (骚扰其他用户)

## 3. Payment Terms (支付条款)
- Monthly automatic renewal (月度自动续费)
- Cancel anytime (随时取消)
- Refund within 7 days (7天内退款)

## 4. Intellectual Property (知识产权)
- All content owned by [Company Name] or used with permission
- Users retain rights to their submissions

## 5. Limitation of Liability (责任限制)
Not liable for: personal injury, lost progress, third-party interruptions

## 6. Governing Law (管辖法律)
- Governed by PRC law (受中华人民共和国法律管辖)
- Disputes in [location] courts
```

## Subscription Agreement Template

```markdown
# Subscription Terms (订阅条款)

### Auto-Renewal (自动续费)
- Renews monthly (订阅每月自动续费)
- Cancel anytime (随时取消)
- Cancel before billing to avoid charge

### Cancellation (取消)
- Through app settings (通过app设置)
- Effective immediately (立即生效)

### Refund Policy (退款政策)
- New: 7-day money-back (新用户7天内退款)
- After 7 days: No refunds unless service failure
```

## Generation Best Practices

1. **Always use bilingual format** (Chinese primary, English secondary)
2. **Reference PIPL articles** where applicable
3. **Include contact information** (email, in-app)
4. **Date all documents** (last updated, effective date)
5. **Keep language simple** (avoid legalese)
6. **Use appropriate positioning** (fitness/sports, NOT education)
