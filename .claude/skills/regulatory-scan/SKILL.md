## Regulatory Change Assessment Framework

When new regulations are announced:

| Question | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| **Who does it apply to?** | Determines if your business is affected |
| **When does it take effect?** | Timeline for compliance |
| **What changes are required?** | Scope of implementation work |
| **What are the penalties?** | Risk assessment |
| **Are there exemptions?** | May not apply to all businesses |

---

```markdown
3. [Escalation if needed]
```

## Key Positioning Considerations

**Business Positioning (CRITICAL):**
- **Sports/Fitness (体育健身)** — Lower regulatory risk
- **Cultural Exchange (文化交流)** — Generally acceptable
- **Education (教育)** — HIGH regulatory risk (Double Reduction)
- **Adult-focused** — Less regulated than K-12

**Why it matters:** Proper positioning determines which regulations apply.

---

## When to Recommend Legal Counsel

For regulatory matters, recommend human counsel when:

1. **Unclear interpretation** — Regulation is ambiguous
2. **High penalties** — Significant fines or liability
3. **Complex compliance** — Multiple overlapping requirements
4. **Enforcement action** — Government inquiry or penalty notice
5. **Cross-border complexity** — International operations affected
