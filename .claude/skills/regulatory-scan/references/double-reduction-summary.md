# Double Reduction Policy Summary

**Source:** https://www.gov.cn/zhengce/2021-07/24/content_5627132.htm
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## Double Reduction Policy (2021-2025)

**Official Title:** 关于进一步减轻义务教育阶段学生作业负担和校外培训负担的意见
**Date:** July 24, 2021
**Issued by:** 中共中央办公厅, 国务院办公厅

---

## Core Regulations

### A. Homework Management

| Grade Level | Maximum Homework |
|-------------|------------------|
| Grades 1-2 | **No written homework** |
| Grades 3-6 | **60 minutes** daily |
| Junior High | **90 minutes** daily |

**Prohibitions:**
- Cannot assign homework to parents
- Cannot require parents to check/grade homework
- Eliminate mechanical, repetitive, punitive homework

### B. Off-Campus Training Restrictions

**Subject-Based Tutoring:**
- No **new approvals** for subject-based tutoring institutions
- Existing institutions must convert to **non-profit** status
- Cannot go public or accept foreign investment
- **No tutoring** on weekends, national holidays, winter/summer vacation
- No advanced/accelerated teaching
- **Online limits:** 30 minutes max/session, ending by 9:00 PM

### C. After-School Services ("5+2" Model)
- Schools must provide after-school services **5 days/week**, **2+ hours daily**
- **100% coverage** of compulsory education schools
- End time not earlier than normal work hours
- Cannot teach new curriculum
- Focus on homework tutoring, interest activities, clubs

---

## Implementation Results (2021-2025)

| Area | Achievement |
|------|-------------|
| Offline tutoring institutions | 96% reduction (124,000 → 4,932) |
| Online tutoring | 87.1% reduction (263 → 34) |
| Homework time reduction | 87.8% of students report reduction |
| After-school service coverage | 100% schools, 85%+ students |
| Training costs | 42% average reduction vs 2021 |
| Myopia rate | Reduced 4.2 percentage points |
| Physical fitness excellence | 56.8% rate achieved |

## Impact on Platform Classification

### Why This Matters for Non-Education Platforms

**Business positioning determines which regulations apply:**

| Positioning | Risk Level | Regulations |
|-------------|------------|-------------|
| **Sports/Fitness (体育健身)** | LOW | General consumer protection |
| **Cultural Exchange (文化交流)** | LOW | General consumer protection |
| **Education (教育)** | HIGH | Double Reduction + licensing |

**⚠️ Critical:** Fitness/physical training platforms should **never** use "Education" classification to avoid Double Reduction regulations.

---

## Safe Positioning for Fitness Platforms

### ✅ Use
- "Fitness practice platform" (健身练习平台)
- "Training studio" (训练工作室)
- "Workout guides" (锻炼指南)
- "Physical training" (体育锻炼)

### ❌ Avoid
- "Online courses" (在线课程)
- "Education platform" (教育平台)
- "Learning center" (学习中心)
- "Tutoring" (辅导)
- "Teaching" (教学) - in academic context
