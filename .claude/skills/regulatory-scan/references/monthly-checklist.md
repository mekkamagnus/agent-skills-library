# Monthly Regulatory Monitoring Checklist

**Source:** Derived from government monitoring best practices
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## Monthly Regulatory Compliance Checklist

---

## Weekly Quick Scan (5 minutes)

| Task | Source | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Check government announcements | NPC, CAC, MOE websites | ⬜ |
| Review platform policy updates | WeChat Developers | ⬜ |
| Scan industry news | Caixin, SCMP | ⬜ |

**Alert triggers for immediate action:**
- New laws effective within 30 days
- Platform policy changes affecting submission
- Data breach rules with new requirements
- Enforcement actions in your industry

---

## Monthly Review (30-60 minutes)

### 1. Government Announcements

**Websites to check:**
- [ ] NPC (https://flk.npc.gov.cn/) - New legislation
- [ ] CAC (https://www.cac.gov.cn/) - Data/cyber regulations
- [ ] MIIT (https://www.miit.gov.cn/) - ICP, internet regulations
- [ ] MOE (https://www.moe.gov.cn/) - Education policy updates
- [ ] SAMR (https://www.samr.gov.cn/) - Consumer protection

**What to look for:**
- New laws or amendments
- Implementation guidance for existing laws
- Enforcement actions
- Consultation drafts (future regulations)

---

### 2. Platform Policy Updates

**WeChat Mini Program:**
- [ ] Review policy page: https://developers.weixin.qq.com/miniprogram/product/
- [ ] Check category classifications
- [ ] Review prohibited content lists
- [ ] Check API changes

**What to look for:**
- Category classification changes
- New prohibited content
- Submission requirement changes
- Payment policy updates
- Privacy requirement changes

---

### 3. Industry News Scan

**Sources:**
- [ ] South China Morning Post (tech/regulation section)
- [ ] Caixin Global (policy section)
- [ ] Sixth Tone (society/policy)
- [ ] China Law Blog (legal analysis)

**What to look for:**
- Regulatory changes affecting your sector
- Enforcement actions against similar platforms
- New interpretation of existing regulations
- Industry best practices

---

### 4. Internal Documentation Review

**Check and update:**
- [ ] Privacy policy - Is it current?
- [ ] Terms of service - Any new requirements?
- [ ] Category classification - Still correct?
- [ ] Positioning language - Accurate?
- [ ] Content guidelines - Up to date?

---

## Quarterly Assessment (2-3 hours)

### 1. Comprehensive Regulatory Review

| Area | Questions | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Data Privacy** | Any PIPL updates? New CAC guidance? Cross-border changes? | |
| **Platform Rules** | WeChat policy changes? New categories? Review process changes? | |
| **Business Positioning** | Still Sports/Fitness (not Education)? Any language issues? | |
| **Content Standards** | New prohibited content? Moderation requirements? | |
| **Payment/Fees** | WeChat Pay changes? Refund policy updates? | |

### 2. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Current Level | Action Required |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Regulatory change impact | Low/Medium/High | |
| Platform suspension risk | Low/Medium/High | |
| Content violation risk | Low/Medium/High | |
| Data compliance gap | Low/Medium/High | |

### 3. Update Internal Documentation

Based on quarterly review:
- [ ] Update privacy policy if needed
- [ ] Update terms of service if needed
- [ ] Update content guidelines if needed
- [ ] Update team on regulatory changes
- [ ] Adjust positioning language if needed

---

## Annual Deep Dive (Full day)

### 1. Complete Regulatory Audit
- Review all laws affecting your platform
- Assess full compliance
- Identify gaps
- Plan remediation

### 2. Legal Counsel Consultation
- Review significant regulatory changes
- Assess contract compliance
- Discuss upcoming regulatory trends

### 3. Training & Documentation
- Train team on regulatory requirements
- Update internal compliance handbook
- Document compliance procedures

## Escalation Triggers

**Immediately escalate to legal counsel for:**
1. New laws affecting your core business
2. Enforcement actions in your industry
3. Platform suspension or warning
4. Data breach or compliance investigation
5. Significant policy changes
6. Unclear regulatory interpretation
