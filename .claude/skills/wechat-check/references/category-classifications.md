# WeChat Mini Program Category Classifications

**Source:** https://open.weixin.qq.com/
**Last Accessed:** 2025-02-28
**Language:** English

## WeChat Mini Program Service Categories

---

## Low-Risk Categories (Recommended)

### Sports/Fitness (体育健身) ⭐
- **Risk Level:** LOW
- **Review Time:** 1-3 days
- **License Required:** No
- **Use For:**
  - Fitness training
  - Physical exercise guides
  - Workout videos
  - Sports practice
  - Health/wellness (non-medical)
- **Examples:**
  - "Daily Fitness Practice"
  - "Workout Training Studio"
  - "Sports Training Guide"

### Cultural Exchange (文化交流) ⭐
- **Risk Level:** LOW
- **Review Time:** 1-3 days
- **License Required:** No
- **Use For:**
  - Cultural content sharing
  - Traditional arts/crafts
  - Cultural education (non-academic)
  - Language exchange
- **Examples:**
  - "Traditional Culture Sharing"
  - "Cultural Art Exchange"

### Social (社交) ⭐
- **Risk Level:** LOW
- **Review Time:** 1-3 days
- **License Required:** No (for basic features)
- **Use For:**
  - Community features
  - User interaction
  - Group activities
- **Examples:**
  - "Fitness Community"
  - "Practice Group"

### E-commerce (电商) ⭐
- **Risk Level:** LOW
- **Review Time:** 1-3 days
- **License Required:** Business license
- **Use For:**
  - Product sales
  - Service bookings
  - Physical goods
- **Examples:**
  - "Fitness Equipment Shop"
  - "Sports Gear Store"

---

## High-Risk Categories (Avoid Without License)

### Education (教育) ⚠️
- **Risk Level:** HIGH
- **Review Time:** 7+ days
- **License Required:** YES (教育办学许可证)
- **Use For:**
  - Academic tutoring
  - K-12 subject training
  - Exam preparation
- **Regulations:**
  - Double Reduction Policy applies
  - Strict oversight
  - Weekends/holidays banned for subject tutoring

---

## Positioning Language Guide

### ❌ Avoid (Triggers Education Classification)
- "在线课程" (Online courses)
- "培训班" (Training class)
- "教育平台" (Education platform)
- "学校" (School)
- "教学" (Teaching)
- "学习" (Learning) - in academic context

### ✅ Use (Fitness Classification)
- "健身练习" (Fitness practice)
- "训练工作室" (Training studio)
- "健身平台" (Fitness platform)
- "练习馆" (Practice studio)
- "训练" (Training)
- "练习" (Practice)

---

## Decision Tree

```
What is your PRIMARY function?
    ↓
Is it K-12 academic tutoring?
    ↓ YES → Education (requires license) ⚠️
    ↓ NO
Is it physical fitness/sports training?
    ↓ YES → Sports/Fitness ⭐
    ↓ NO
Is it product sales?
    ↓ YES → E-commerce ⭐
    ↓ NO
Is it cultural content sharing?
    ↓ YES → Cultural Exchange ⭐
    ↓ NO
Is it community/interaction focused?
    ↓ YES → Social ⭐
    ↓ NO
    Default: Sports/Fitness (if fitness-adjacent)
```

---

## Important Notes

1. **Category is based on PRIMARY function** - not all features
2. **Mixed content** - Choose category matching 60%+ of content
3. **Can be changed** - Resubmit if incorrectly categorized
4. **Positioning matters** - Language affects classification
