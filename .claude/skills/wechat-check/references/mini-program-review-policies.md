**Language:** English

## WeChat Mini Program Review Standards

---

## Review Timeline

| Category | Typical Review Time | Notes |
|----------|---------------------|-------|
| Sports/Fitness (体育健身) | 1-3 days | Low risk, faster review |
| Cultural Exchange (文化交流) | 1-3 days | Low risk, faster review |
| Education (教育) | 7+ days | High risk, requires license |
| Social (社交) | 1-3 days | Standard review |
| E-commerce (电商) | 1-3 days | Standard review |

---

## Content Requirements

### 1. Functional Completeness
- Mini Program must have **complete functionality**
- Cannot be a single page or simple button
- Must provide actual service value
- Test thoroughly before submission

### 2. Content Categories
Select appropriate category based on PRIMARY function:
- **Sports/Fitness (体育健身)**: Physical training, workouts, fitness guidance
- **Cultural Exchange (文化交流)**: Cultural content sharing
- **Education (教育)**: Academic tutoring (REQUIRES license)
- **Social (社交)**: Community features
- **E-commerce (电商)**: Product sales

⚠️ **Critical:** For fitness/physical training platforms, **always use Sports/Fitness** — NOT Education.

### 3. Required Pages
- Homepage (首页)
- Feature/Service pages
- Privacy Policy (隐私政策)
- Terms of Service (服务条款)
- Contact/About (联系我们/关于我们)

---

## Submission Checklist

### Basic Requirements
- [ ] AppID registered and verified
- [ ] App Name matches business registration
- [ ] Service Category selected (Sports/Fitness for fitness apps)
- [ ] Privacy Policy URL accessible in-app
- [ ] Terms of Service URL accessible in-app
- [ ] Content Description accurate and complete
- [ ] Screenshots (3-5) showing core features
- [ ] Testing completed on real devices

### Content Requirements
- [ ] Sufficient content (not blank or minimal)
- [ ] No prohibited content
- [ ] No inducement to share
- [ ] No excessive advertising
- [ ] No test/divination content
- [ ] No fraudulent content

## Common Rejection Reasons

| Rejection Message | Cause | Fix |
|------------------|-------|-----|
| "类别不符" (Category mismatch) | Wrong category selected | Re-submit under correct category |
| "内容不足" (Insufficient content) | Too few pages/content | Add FAQ, About, Contact pages |
| "隐私政策缺失" (Privacy policy missing) | No accessible privacy policy | Add accessible link |
| "诱导分享" (Inducement to share) | Prompts to share content | Remove share prompts |
| "功能过于简单" (Functions too simple) | Single page/minimal features | Add more functionality |
| "涉及教育类需资质" (Education requires license) | Education category without license | Switch to Sports/Fitness |

## Appeal Process

### If Your Mini Program Is Suspended

1. **Read suspension notice carefully**
   - Understand the specific reason
   - Note the violation reference

2. **Check category classification**
   - Verify Sports/Fitness, not Education
   - Review content positioning

3. **Review content for violations**
   - Scan for prohibited content
   - Check for inducement/spam
   - Verify functionality completeness

4. **Fix identified issues**
   - Make necessary changes
   - Test thoroughly

5. **Submit appeal**
   - Use WeChat MP backend appeal process
   - Provide explanation of fixes
   - Include evidence of corrections

6. **Wait for review**
   - Typically 1-7 days
   - May request additional information

## Annual Certification

Since September 2025:
- All Mini Programs require **annual certification**
- Deadline: Based on registration date
- Failure: Temporary suspension until certified
